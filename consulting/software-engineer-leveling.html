Software Engineer Leveling

As organizations grow, engineers start to ask questions about where their careers are going, and about potential growth opportunities at your company. As an engineering leader, you have the opportunity to add structure and to guide engineers into future roles that benefit each of them and your organization.

First, a growth framework & leveling matrix for your engineers

  • Define competencies, roles & levels specific to your culture
  • Detail level limits, well-roundedness expectations, and other requirements
  • Teach engineers & managers alike how to navigate the levels

Then, career paths within & across functions

  • Give engineers concrete examples of supported paths on your teams
  • Address at least one technical track, a managerial track, and cross-functional opportunities
  • Provide tools for individuals, for path planning & transitions

Finally, active career development with your managers

  • Empower managers to lead career development with vision
  • Bootstrap managers with specific tools for career development
  • Coach managers on the most common desires & transition points
