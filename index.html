About the Principal

My name is Bradford Fults and I have over 25 years of experience in large-scale B2C and B2B enterprise organizations, in both software-native and tech-enabled services businesses, and from the smallest teams up to integrated cross-product portfolio delivery teams of 1,000 people.

I bring a sharp eye, effective practices, and proven teaching and change management methods to achieve the lasting change you seek in your business, all the way to the balance sheet. I am an accomplished leader of organizations and technically-minded engineer, able to quickly assess both people and systems and then to forecast and integrate the capabilities of product & engineering with the rest of the business.

Approach

  • I bring a customer-driven mindset to product development and engineering (software & hardware), focusing first and foremost on how successfully your team is delivering actual results to customers that power the engine of the business, in partnership with your go-to-market teams.
  • I bring only real-world practices that have been used in real businesses to get great results while running on actual budgets — no big-acronym frameworks or slick slide decks without effective methods behind them.
  • I lead with a people-centered approach because the most sustainable and effective teams are tightly-knit and trusting teams with great leaders and excellent cross-functional relationships. It is these teams who will deliver better products, more quickly, and who will be more responsive to the needs of your customers and the business.
